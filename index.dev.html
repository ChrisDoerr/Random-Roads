Random Roads: Game Rules

You start at the top left position and have to drive your vehicle to the exit, which is always located at the bottom right of the map.

The vehicle will drive on its own but you have to stear, using the W, A, S, D keys on your keyboard.

When your vehicle hits a wall you die and (if you have enought lives left) have to replay the current level.

When your vehicle exits a map you will level-up and become faster and faster.

Since the maps are generated randomly it might happen that your vehicle or the exit is walled in, leaving you no other choice than to die. To compansate for that inconvinience, you have 7 lives for each level. Meaning, each time you level up, your number of lives will be reset!
